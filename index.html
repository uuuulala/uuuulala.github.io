What I do

I do front-end development, mainly focusing on 3D web experiences and 2D animations, such as:
  • Product configurators
  • Scroll-driven animations
  • Interactive maps & infographics
  • Animated logos, loaders, and typography
  • Data visualizations
  • Interactive cursors
  • Particles animations
  • Browser games
In terms of tech, my main tools are WebGL (Three.js & GLSL Shaders), SVG and HTML5 Canvas, and JS libs like GSAP, d3.js, cannon-es, etc. Here you can see some examples of my work.

Who I am

I'm Ksenia Kondrashova, freelance front-end developer focused on motion design, animations, and visualizations. Creative Front-End Developer if you wish :)

My clients are agencies, product companies, as well as with individual developers, designers and entrepreneurs. I'm working with rough concepts, detailed designs, and everything in between.

I have an MA degree in embedded electronics (still love hardware!) but turned to web development a few years after graduation.
